Microsoft Copilot (Microsoft 365): Generate text for a PowerPoint slide using slide context

Copilot in PowerPoint now helps you create a compelling title, a concise summary, or captions for your visuals. Just describe what you want in natural language and Copilot will generate text based on the content of your slide. It’s a simple way to turn ideas into content quickly. Updated May 12, 2026: We have decided not to move forward with this feature at this time. We apologize for the inconvenience.
