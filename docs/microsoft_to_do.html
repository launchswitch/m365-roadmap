Microsoft To Do: Microsoft To Do availability in Outlook on the web

Microsoft To Do will be available from the Outlook on the web app launcher. To Do will replace the tasks module currently available. After this change, you will see To Do in the app launcher instead of tasks.

Status: Launched | GA Date: August CY2025 | Roadmap Status: Include this month

Products: Microsoft To Do, Outlook

Cloud: GCC High, DoD

Platforms: Web

Phase: General Availability

Planner: New Microsoft Planner App for Teams for customers in GCC-H

The new Microsoft Planner app in Teams is a single, unified work management experience. It brings together the simplicity of To Do, the collaboration of Planner and the power of Project into a single and simple solution that offers individual task management, collaborative work management as well as enterprise work management.

Status: In development | GA Date: May CY2025 | Roadmap Status: Include this month

Products: Microsoft Project, Microsoft To Do, Planner

Cloud: GCC High

Platforms: Desktop, Teams and Surface Devices, Web

Phase: General Availability
